DnaJB1 CTDs saturation binding to Hsp72 tracer
-GOAL: repeat this saturation binding experiment (from 031220) with an increased concentration of tracer, to increase dynamic range and decrease relative deviation between replicates
-conditions: 20nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 1mM DTT, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-2-fold protein dilutions were performed, starting at 200uM (16pt dose)
-RESULTS: greater dynamic range (max mP) was achieved, approx IC50 calculated at 4.2uM
