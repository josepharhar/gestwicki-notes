TEV purification:
- GOALS: purify a fresh batch of TEV, because the previous batch was deactivated during the freezer thaw
- TEV was expressed overnight May 2 8:45pm - May 3 8:45am using TB autoinduction media as previously described (see TEV purification protocol)
- TEV was purified as described
- 6 25mL elutions were performed, and protein levels in each fraction was assessed by SDS-PAGE
- the first five elutions were combined, and diluted with Buffer A, glycerol (30% final), and 10X freezing buffer, and were flash frozen in 1mL aliquots
- RESULTS: while a high level of TEV was eluted in wash 2, a high yield of TEV was still acquired by elution
