Make tau fibrils for wt tau, P301L, N279K, K18, and K18 P301L:
- see Sue’s Tau aggregation protocol for tubes (attached)
- order of addition: add MgCl2 to DPBS, filter, add DTT, filter (?), add tau, then add filtered heparin
- to prepare heparin, 8.5 mg was weighed into an eppendorf and dissolved in 1 mL water; 0.5 mL of this solution was then diluted in 9.5 mL assay buffer; 500 uL of this heparin solution was added to each 5 mL solution
- prepared ~5 mL of each tau/assay buffer/heparin solution, and divided into 330 mL aliquots in eppendorf tubes (14-15 samples per tau variant)
- into 37 deg shaker in Prusiner lab for ~36 hrs

Cellular seeding assay—seeding wt tau and P301L cell lines with 0N4R tau fibrils:
- see attached protocol and 384 well plate template—> this template is correct, but upside down (as labeled, the sample in A1 is actually located in P24, etc)
- using at 384 well plate, fill 5 columns with DMEM (1/10 of regular pen/strep supplemented) for each cell line (make 2 duplicate plates); 60 uL media per well
- add 10-40 uL fibril mix, then spin plates at 4000 rpm for 20 min
- dox induce cells and then plate 3000 cells per well
