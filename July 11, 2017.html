Imaging fibril transfected cells:
- removed the excess volume from the top wells (30 and 10 uL respectively)
- from 10 mg/mL stock solution of Hoescht, make a stock solution as a 1:1000 dilution in DMEM with 1/10 pen/strep
- 10 uL of this stock was added to each well, and then cells were allowed to incubate for 1 hr
- all wells were imaged on the in cell

Cold shock treatment:
- goal is to detach tau from the microtubules to (hopefully) improve seeding, particularly in WT cell line
- 2nd plate of fibril transfected cells was placed on ice for 20 min and then immediately returned to the incubator
