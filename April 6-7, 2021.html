Hsp72 Delta EEVD purification
-goal: purify Hsp72 Delta EEVD for JDP-EEVD binding and activity assays, starting from 2L pelleted cultures
-protocol: purify according to Oletaâ€™s Hsc70 purification protocol (see March 23rd), with changes to protocol listed below
  • Ni-NTA resin was washed with 200mL His binding buffer, followed by 200mL His wash buffer
  • for 2L of culture, I used 4 total TEV aliquots (2 per L)
  • protein eluted in fractions 2A2-2B3
  • fractions 2A2 and 2B1-2B3 were combined and concentrated from ~95mL to 35mL total filtered
  • fraction 2A3, which seemed highly concentrated by SDS-PAGE, was kept separate as a more concentrated stock
  • combined fractions were frozen in 30 1-1.5mL aliquots, and 2A3 was frozen in 15 1mL aliquots
  • at the end of the method for the ATP column, flow rate continued, so the run was manually ended
-BCA for protein concentration:
  • 2A3 concentrated aliquots: 32.00uM
  • combined fractions: 8.75uM
