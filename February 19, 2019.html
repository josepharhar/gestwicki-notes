DJA2 Delta CT purification day 4:
- GOAL: identify fractions of interest from SEC75, concentrate, and freeze
- the void, shoulder, and subsequent peaks were analyzed by SDS-PAGE (see attached)
- fractions B3-B5 were concentrated using 10K MWCO concentrators, and the solution was concentrated from 6mL to 2mL before precipitation was observed
- remaining protein was filtered and frozen
- RESULTS: while the majority of DJA2 Delta CT was present in the void, there was also protein present in the second/shoulder peak; this likely corresponds to monomeric protein
