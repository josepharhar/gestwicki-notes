Dialyzing tau stocks overnight:
- samples for dialysis were 3 aliquots of WT 0N4R tau and 3 aliquots of A152T tau, each aliquot 1 mL
- for each of the 3 mL of tau, 1 L of buffer was prepared (rule of thumb: 1 to 40 volumes for dialysis)
- for each liter of buffer prepared, 1 L of chilled DPBS was combined with 1 mL DTT stock solution and 2 mL 1M MgCl2 stock solution, for a final concentration of 1 mM DTT and 2 mM MgCl2
- samples were transferred to 3K dialysis cassettes, then allowed to dialyze in the cold room overnight, with stirring
