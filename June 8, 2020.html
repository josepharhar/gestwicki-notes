DJB1 CTDs competition FP:
-GOAL: perform competition experiments with amidated Hsp72 10mer and Hsp72 ala scan peptides; observe how trends may or may not differ from those observed with FL DJB4 construct
-conditions: 5uM DJB1 CTDs, 20nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 2.5% DMSO, 1mM DTT, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-RESULTS: as observed with FL DJB4 construct, amidated peptide has minimal ability to compete with tracer; P5 isoleucine still seems to be the most critical residue for affinity, although it stands out less from the rest of the alanine scan compared to experiments performed with FL DJB4; P2 alanine substitution performs as well or better than WT peptide at competing with tracer
