DJA1 purification day 4:
- GOALS: concentrate, BCA, and freeze
- relevant dimer fractions (see attached) were pooled and concentrated using 30kDa MWCO spin units
- BCA was performed on filtered, concentrated, and undiluted protein as previously described
- RESULTS: upon concentration to the approximate volume injected onto SEC200, the majority of protein precipitated out of solution, as shown by ~baseline signal by BCA

FKBP12 HSQC:
- GOALS: perform a titration of FKBP12 into labeled K18 to determine dose response/concentration of FKBP12 required for presence of lower right peaks
- FKBP12 was added to labeled K18 at 0.5:1, 1:1, and 1.5:1 molar ratios
- see attached calculations
- RESULTS: even at the lowest tested concentration, the lower right peaks were present; to determine whether those peaks are present due to a catalytic event (versus binding) a lower concentration will have to be tested
