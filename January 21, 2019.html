DnaJA2 Delta CT purification day 1:
- GOAL: purify DnaJA2 Delta CT by affinity using Ni-NTA resin, cleave off 6X-His tag, dialyze into His-binding buffer
- E. coli pellet was resuspended, cells were lysed, and lysate was clarified as described
- lysate was incubated with equilibrated Ni-NTA resin for 1hr at 4C
- resin was washed with 1L of each of the JDP buffers prepared 011719
- elution was performed twice, ~25mL each
- upon of addition of DTT up to 1mM final, ~6 aliquots of TEV protease were added to purified DJA2 Delta CT and allowed to incubate approximately 6hrs at RT
- protein was dialyzed into 4L of His-binding buffer, using 5k MWCO dialysis tubing, allowing to dialyzed with stirring overnight at 4C
- RESULTS: DnaJA2 Delta CT was eluted with high purity, but low yield, by affinity purification (see attached)
