DJA2 native gel with reducing agent:
- GOAL: perform native gel analysis of DJA2 in the presence of increasing concentration of 2-mercaptoethanol (BME), to determine whether reversible oxidation is the source of high order species
- 20uM of batch prep (His purification) DJA2 was loaded into each lane, with the following BME concentrations spiked in: 0, 1, 2.5, 5, and 10mM
- RESULTS: the gel (see attached) suggests that the presence of reducing agent post-purification does not reverse the presence of high-order DJA2 species
