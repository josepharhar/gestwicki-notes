Transfer fibrils to non-binding tubes:
- all samples were combined in 50 mL falcon tubes and then transferred to nonbinding tubes in approx 1.5 mL aliquots
- for one tube of each of the following samples, the original tube was refilled with 330 uL of PBS to test for tube binding: WT, K18, K18 P301L, and K18 P301L no heparin control

Day 2 western blots:
- collected primary Ab and stored in cold room
- rinsed off primary with TBST for 30 min on rocker at RT
- added secondary at a 1:10,000 dilution in 5% milk in TBST and incubated on rocker at RT for 45 min
- imaged with ECL Prime reagent, exposing for up to 7 minutes
- RESULTS: was able to get a pretty good signal for all blots; tubulin and GFP blots had relatively expected ratios (WT has most GFP in microtubule fraction, followed by N279K and then by P301L—however more GFP in N279K cytoplasmic fraction than expected); tau blot had unexpected P301L ratio (MUCH more tau in the microtubule fraction than cytosolic fraction—opposite of expected result), which may be due to switching the sample order—>will strip GFP blot and reblot for tau to confirm results
