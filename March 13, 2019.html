DJB1 purification day 2:
- GOAL: combine SEC200 fractions and concentrate before freezing purified protein
- 10uL of relevant fractions from SEC200 were analyzed by SDS-PAGE
- fractions were combined in two groups; a highest purity sample and a lower purity sample, based on the absence or presence of a low molecular weight contaminant
- RESULTS: a low molecular weight protein contaminant comigrated with DJB1 dimer on the SEC200 column; only fractions that do not contain a high amount of this contaminant were selected for further characterization and use in the GSK DNA-encoded library screen
