DJB4 batch comparisons for FP
-goal: given the low maximum signal observed on 012820, I tested the max signal and competition with 250uM Hsp72 10mer for both 010820 DJB4 batch and freshly dialyzed batch from newest prep
-conditions: 18uL total volume; 5uM DJB4, 10nM Hsp72 10mer tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 0.01% Triton, pH 8; +/- 250uM Hsp72 10mer peptide
-results: 010820 DJB4 batch indeed has a lower maximum mP value for the same amount of tracer; newest batch (021220) has similar max mP to previous DJB4 batch (~140-150mP); both batches show competition with the peptide; however, it is unclear if the 010820 batch has a lower max mP, OR if its affinity for the tracer is different than the prior batch
