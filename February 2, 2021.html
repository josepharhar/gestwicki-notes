Replicates of DJB4 competition with Hsp70 and Hsp90 10mer peptides:
-GOAL: perform replicate experiment for JDP-EEVD paper
-caveat: unfortunately, the peptide stocks were not all at the same concentration, so there is slight variation in %DMSO at highest peptide concentrations; in the future, fresh peptide stocks will be made at matching concentrations
-conditions: 5uM DJB4, 10nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 1mM DTT, 2.5% DMSO, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-total assay volume: 18uL
-controls: no peptide, no peptide or protein (aka tracer + buffer)
-after combining all reagents, plate was incubated in the dark at room temp for 30 min
-peptides were tested by 3-fold dilutions
-data normalization: 0% tracer bound is set by the average mP from triplicate tracer alone control; 100% tracer bound is defined as the largest mean in each data set (presumably, the largest mean mP comes from the no peptide control)
-RESULTS: Hsp72 and Hsc70 peptides potently competed with p72 tracer; Hsp90 peptides showed little to no competition. Upon data normalization, Hsc70 peptide yielded a negative % tracer bound; this may be due to non matching %DMSO and will have to be retested to confirm
