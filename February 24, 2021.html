DJB4 competition FP troubleshooting:

-GOAL: given the large variance between replicates observed on Feb 18, a small scale experiment will be performed with only a positive and negative control (WT 10mer and animated 10mer respectively)
-Experimental design:
  • conditions: 5uM DJB4, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 1mM DTT, 2.5% DMSO, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
  • peptides were tested at the four highest concentrations from Feb 18th (250uM max, 3-fold dilutions)
  • to assess the stability of the fluorophore, fluorescence intensity was also recorded
  • to account for plate reader variability, two plate readers were used to measure the same plate, with minimal time between reads
  • in case variability between replicates was introduced by the matrix pipette, only the lower four tips were used (as opposed to the top four, which I would normally use for the highest peptide concentrations)
-RESULTS: 
  • while major outliers were not observed in this experiment, there was less separation between the positive and negative controls than I have previously observed;
  • when the plate was measured on the second plate reader (Cain), the negative control amidated peptide strikingly displayed competition with the tracer—not good
  • fluorescence intensity was relatively constant across all samples; slightly higher intensity was observed for the fluorophore alone, suggesting that interaction with DJB4 may modify overall fluorescence
