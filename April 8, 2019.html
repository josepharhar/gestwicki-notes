DJA1 expression:
- GOAL: grow 4L of DJA1 Rosetta cells, induce DJA1 expression and allow to grow overnight
- to each of 4L of TB, added 1mL of 20mM ZnCl2 stock to yield a final concentration of 20uM, 10mL of overnight culture, and 1mL of 50mg/mL kan stock
- incubated at 37C, 180 RPM until OD reached ~0.8
- allowed to cool for 1hr to 18C, 160RPM
- induced each L of culture with 500uL of 1M IPTG stock, allowed to incubate overnight
- prior to induction, 1mL of culture was pelleted and stored at -20C for future assessment of expression
