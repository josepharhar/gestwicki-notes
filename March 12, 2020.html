DnaJB1 CTDs saturation binding to Hsp72 10mer tracer:
-GOAL: perform saturation binding to DJB1 CTDs, to determine approx EC50 and design competition FP parameters
-conditions: 10nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 1mM DTT, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-2-fold protein dilutions were performed, starting at 200uM (16pt dose)
-results: it seems that saturation was reached; however, total mP at saturation was notably lower than that for DJB4 FL, and in this smaller dynamic range greater standard deviations were observed; approximate EC50 from one site-total curve fitting was 8.7uM, which is greater than that of DJB4 FL (~3uM)
