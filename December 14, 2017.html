WT tau purification day 1:
- see protocol attached Dec 12 for details
- resuspension buffer was prepared, including DTT; however, PMSF was always added directly to the solution in use, instead of into the stock solution (ex: after adding 30 mL resuspension buffer to the lysate, the appropriate volume of PMSF stock was added directly to the lysate/resuspension buffer solution
- 3L pellet was resuspended in approx 40 mL resuspension buffer
- after homogenization and rinsing of the eppendorf, volume was approx 80 mL—>added approx 20 mL resuspension buffer to make sure that starting volume for microfluidizer was at least 100mL total
- total volume after microfluidizer was still approx 100mL, so 10mL of 5M NaCl (filtered) was added directly to the lysate
- lysate was stored in an E-flask, which was then submerged in vigorously boiling water for 20-30 min (NOTE: it took a very long time to get water vigorously boiling—>required setting heat on hot plate to >300C); after that time, some separation was visible
- after pelleting (as described), supernatants were yellowish in color
- supernatants were transferred to dialysis tubing (3.5kDa cutoff); though total volume fell within recommended range for 4L dialysis buffer, we split the sample (approx 100mL total), dialyzing approx 50mL per 4L buffer
