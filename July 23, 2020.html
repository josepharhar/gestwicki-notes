JDP competition FP with FL Hsc70:
-GOAL: compete Hsp72 tracer away from DJB1 CTDs or DJB4 with Hsc70
-Hsc70 was concentrated to maximize highest concentration in assay; BCA performed, [Hsc70] = 176.54uM
-Hsc70 was diluted in 7pt 2-fold dilution series; highest concentration in assay = 147.12uM
-RESULTS: Hsc70 competed away the tracer from both JDPs; at 36.78uM Hsc70, polarization reached a minimum (DJB4) or a plateau; at highest concentration of Hsc70, polarization of tracer is slightly increased over tracer alone control (see bar graph)
-unexpected behaviors: increased variance between replicates at highest Hsc70 concentrations; in the case of DJB4, higher concentrations of Hsc70 actually increased polarization, indicative of increased binding to/interaction with tracer
