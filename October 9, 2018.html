Subcloning Young Lab DJA2 constructs from pCDNA3.1 into pMCSG7:
- GOALs: 
  1. PCR amplify the genes of interest from pCDNA3.1, adding TEV cleavage site, stop codon, and KpnI and SacI restriction sites, respectively
  2. Run diagnostic gel to determine the success of the PCR
  3. PCR cleanup
  4. Restriction digest of PCR products and empty pMCSG7 vector using KpnI and SacI-HF
  5. CIP treatment of vector to dephosphorylate ends
  6. Gel purification of each restriction digest
  7. Ligation
- primers were resuspended and diluted as described (see attached from IDT)
- 50uL PCR followed guidelines from NEB protocol (attached), master mix was assembled as described
PCR Protocol for Phusion High-Fidelity DNA Polymerase (M0530) _ NEB.pdf
- due to high annealing temperature of the designed primers, a 2-step PCR was employed as follows:
  1. 98C, 30 sec
  2. 35 cycles of 98C, 10 sec followed by 72C, 45 sec
  3. 72C, 7 min
  4. 4C holding temp
- amplification was successful, as shown in diagnostic gel (see attached), with lanes from left to right: ladder, DJA2-2-1, DJA1-2-2, DJA2 Delta M2, DJA2 CT
