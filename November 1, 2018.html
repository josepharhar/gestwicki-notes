DJA221 and DJC17 purification day 3:
- see attached for details
- DJC17 was dialyzed into 1X his binding buffer for 4 hrs at 4C
- DJA221 SEC200 peaks were identified and separated as described
- DJA221 was concentrated, using 30kDa MWCO spin columns; however, at a total volume higher than 10 mL (which was the original injection volume for SEC), the protein started crashing out—looked like thin crystalline strings, not like a white precipitate
- BCA was then performed, but protein concentrations were too low for most biochemical assays
- to aid in concentration, glycerol was spiked into the respective DJA221 solutions to 10% final by volume; however, despite mixing this led to more protein crashing out
- after iterative rounds of filtering, concentrating, and crashing, proteins were stored on ice until final BCA and freezing
- after dialysis, DJC17 was purified using 10mL packed Ni-NTA resin; washes were performed with a modified buffer (500mL of 50mM Tris, 750mM NaCl, NO IMIDAZOLE PRESENT)
- DJC17 was successfully eluted off of the resin, with a few (~3) noticeable contaminating bands
- after concentration of DJC17 using 10kDa MWCO spin columns, BCA was performed for the two proteins
- final DJC17 concentration: ~74uM
- final DJA221 concentration: ~5uM—this is not concentrated enough for use in biochemical assays
