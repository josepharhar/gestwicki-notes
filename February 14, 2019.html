DJA2 Delta CT purification day 1:
- GOAL: purify DJA2 Delta CT according to JDP purification protocol, for use in future HSQC experiments; day 1 purify by affinity to Ni-NTA resin, TEV cleave His tag, and dialyze into His-binding buffer for reverse TEV purification
- change to protocol: wash 2.5 (with the presence of ADP) was skipped during this purification
- note: elution samples tested by SDS-PAGE had already been allowed to TEV cleave for ~15min at RT before loaded onto gel (this may be a reason for multiple bands in elution fractions—see attached)
- RESULTS: DJA2 Delta CT was isolated with relative purity, given the assumptions that multiple bands were multiple DJA2 Delta CT species in the process of TEV cleavage
