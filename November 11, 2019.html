DnaJB4 DSF:
- GOAL: screen through a salt gradient (+/- DMSO) for DJB4 + Hsp72 10mer peptide
- conditions: all 15uL total reactions, 10uM DJB4, 100uM Hsp72 10mer, 15-50mM NaCl, 0.01% Triton, 0.2% or 5% DMSO
- change to conditions: the wells that were meant to be 5% DMSO controls (protein only) ended up with only 0.2% DMSO, except for 15mM NaCl protein only control
- RESULTS: a small trend of increasing dTm with decreasing salt was observed; however, dTm’s don’t vary greatly between conditions; while best DJB1 condition showed dTm ~3 degrees, best observed here for DJB4 was ~1.5 degrees
