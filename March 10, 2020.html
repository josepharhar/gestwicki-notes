DJB4 competition FP:
-GOAL: test remaining 9 isoleucine positional scan peptides for binding to DJB4; retest GSGPRIEEVD Hsp70/90 swap peptide, and test highest concentrations of WT Hsp72 10mer as a control
-conditions: 5uM DJB4, 10nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 2.5% DMSO, 1mM DTT, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-DJB4 stock (in 50mM Tris, 300mM NaCl, 10% glycerol, pH 8) was directly diluted into the assay buffer
-tracer only control was prepared in buffer that also included a dilution of the above DJB4 buffer
-Hsp72 WT 10mer was tested at the 4 highest concentrations (250uM, 83uM, 27uM, 9uM)
-results: high variability was observed, particularly at high peptide concentrations—this could very likely be due to peptide aggregation, which is likely to be spontaneous and vary from well to well; broadly, none of the tested peptides seemed to have higher affinity than the WT sequence; as expected, the valine substitution was the most similar to the WT; notably, phenylalanine was accommodated, though it didn’t have the same affinity as the WT; GSGPRIEEVD behaved the same as the WT sequence until the highest peptide concentration
