DJA1 purification day 2:
- GOAL: reverse purify TEV and further purify DJA1 by running on SEC200
- DJA1 was present in the elution (see attached), suggesting that not all of it was cleaved by TEV or was sticking nonspecifically to the resin
- upon concentration in 30K MWCO centrifugal unit, there was immediate precipitation of white flakes; samples were removed and filtered, and then glycerol concentration was increased to 10%
- upon repeated precipitation, samples were again removed, filtered, and the glycerol concentration was increased to approx 20%
- upon further centrifugation, there was still precipitation, but it was minimal, and the samples were then filtered before injection onto SEC200 column equilibrated with modified JDP buffer ( 50mM Tris pH 8, 300mM NaCl, 5mM BME, 20uM ZnCl2, 5% glycerol)
- RESULTS: the entirety of the protein signal eluted off of the column in the void volume (see attached)
