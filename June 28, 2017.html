Induction and expression:
- to each liter of autoclaved media, 15 mL of overnight culture and 1 mL ampicillin stock (100 mg/mL) were added
- incubated at 37 degrees, starting at 10 AM
- at approx 2 PM, OD was 0.7, so cultures were moved to cold room incubator and incubated at 18 degrees, 160 rpm for 1 hr
- 1M IPTG stock was prepared (~476mg / 2mL), and 500 uL was added to each liter of culture at 3 PM
- continued incubation conditions overnight

Glycerol stock:
- 3 mL of Sue’s LB media was combined with 3 uL of 100 mg/mL ampicillin stock,  and then inoculated with Rosetta cell glycerol stock
- incubated at 37 degrees, starting at 10 AM
- at approx 5 PM, culture was removed from incubator, and in 3 aliquots, 0.9 mL of culture was combined with 0.9 mL of 60% glycerol to make final glycerol stock concentrations of 30%
- cells were immediately frozen at -80 degrees
