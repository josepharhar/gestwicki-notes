DJB1 and DJB4 FP with Hsp72 C-term tracer:
- GOAL: saturate binding of DJB1 and DJB4 to relevant tracer to determine Kd (this will allow for competition experiments)
- JDPs were tested in two-fold dilutions, starting from highest possible concentration (determined yesterday)
- due to insufficient DJB1 volume, DJB1 was diluted down to a starting concentration of 221.5 (see attached details)
- final assay conditions: 20nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 50mM NaCl, 0.01% Triton, pH 8, variable protein concentration
- RESULTS: DJB4 binding was nearly saturated, with a Kd of ~15uM; DJB1 was not as close to saturation
