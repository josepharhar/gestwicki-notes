DJA1 expression:
- after addition of 1mL 50mg/mL kan to each prepared L of TB, 10mL of O/N culture was added
- incubated at 37C, 180RPM
- when OD600 reached 0.790, cultures were moved to 18C, 160RPM for 1 hr
- 1mL of culture was collected for gel analysis (max speed centrifugation for 1 min, pellet stored at -20C), and then 500uL of 1M IPTG stock was added to each L of culture
- cultures were then incubated in the same conditions overnight
- see attached for details

DJB1 purification:
- two sets of DJB1 fractions were pooled, as described; one set had higher purity, and the other had more of the ~27-30kDa impurity
- fractions were concentrated using 30MWCO spin columns, decreasing the volume of each set from ~6mL to ~2-2.5mL

DJB1 BCA:
