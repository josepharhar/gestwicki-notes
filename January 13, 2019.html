Transformation of EB3 plasmid into Nova Blue and Rosetta competent cells:
- GOALS: transform into (1) Nova Blue cells to propagate EB3 pMCSG7 plasmid for future sequencing/use and (2) Rosetta cells to troubleshoot EB3 expression
- to 1 aliquot of Nova Blue cells, added 0.5uL of EB3 pMCSG7 plasmid; to 1 aliquot of Rosetta cells, added ~0.5uL (perhaps less) of EB3 pMCSG7 plasmid
- incubated on ice 5 min, heat shock 42C 45 sec, recovered on ice 2 min, added 1mL LB and allowed to incubate at 37C, 250RPM for 20 min
- for each transformation mixture, 50uL was plated onto prewarmed amp plates and allowed to incubate at 37C overnight
