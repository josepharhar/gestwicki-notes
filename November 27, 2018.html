Troubleshooting ATPase assay:
- GOAL: prepare fresh reagents, and test a gradient of sodium citrate to find a cause for signal saturation and/or a temporary solution
- the following reagents were freshly prepared: 250mM NaPi, malachite green buffer (pH 7.4)
- a 2-fold dilution series of sodium citrate was tested in duplicate to determine which conditions maintain linear signal
- RESULTS: all tested conditions maintained a linear signal except for 32% w/v sodium citrate; however, the overall signal was lower for each of these conditions than the measured signal for the controls on the last time that this experiment worked (110618); perhaps the interim solution is to find a solution somewhere between 32% and 16% that is still linear but produces a larger signal
