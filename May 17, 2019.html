DJA1 expression:
- GOAL: match the exact expression conditions published by Nillegoda et al. 2015
- once OD600 ~0.8 was reached, incubators were cooled to 30C, slowed to 160RPM, and cultures were induced with 500mM IPTG
- 1 mL samples were removed every hour to determine the level of expression
- after 3 hours the 2L cultures were pelleted at 4000RPM and stored at -80C
- uninduced and induced samples were pelleted, resuspended in 1X SDS sample buffer, boiled for 5 min, and then centrifuged at max speed for 5 min before running on an SDS-PAGE gel (note: expression from May 10 cultures was also assessed by these means)
- RESULTS: expression was observed at all three time points, suggesting that for future work, 1hr expression may be sufficient
