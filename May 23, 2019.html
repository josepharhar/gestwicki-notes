Cloning DJA2 CTDI:
- GOAL: use PCR and primers ordered 051519 to clone out J domain and linker region from DJA2 Delta CT pMCSG7, leaving only a CTDI pMCSG7 construct
- primers were resuspended in nuclease-free water at the volumes described in the spec sheets (see attached 051519)
- resuspended primers were then diluted 1:10 to final concentration 10uM
- assembled the total PCR reaction as follows:
  1. 1.73uL of 5.77 ng/uL DJA2 Delta CT pMCSG7 plasmid stock (10 ng total)
  2. 2.5uL 10uM forward primer
  3. 2.5uL 10uM reverse primer
  4. 10uL HF buffer
  5. 1uL dNTPs (10mM)
  6. 31.77uL water
  7. 0.5uL Phusion
- set up the following PCR cycle:
  1. 98C - 30 sec
  2. 98C - 10 sec
  3. 72C - 2 min 50 sec
  4. Repeat 2-3 30X
  5. 72C - 10 min
  6. 4C - storage
- added 1uL of DpnI to total PCR reaction and incubated at 37C for 1 hr (to removed bacterial/methylated DNA)
- transformed 10uL of PCR reaction into competent cells, plated on amp plate
