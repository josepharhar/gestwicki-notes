Prepare buffers for DJA1 purification:
- GOAL: replicate conditions published by Nillegoda et al. 2015
- buffers were prepared as described:
  1. lysis buffer: 50mM HEPES, 750mM KCl, 5mM MgCl2, 10% glycerol, 6mM BME, pH 7.5 (PMSF and protease inhibitor cocktail to be supplemented freshly)
  2. wash 1: 50mM HEPES, 750mM KCl, 5mM MgCl2, 10% glycerol, 6mM BME, pH 7.5
  3. wash 2: 50mM HEPES, 500mM KCl, 5mM MgCl2, 10% glycerol, 6mM BME, pH 7.5
  4. elution buffer: 50mM HEPES, 500mM KCl, 300mM imidazole, 5mM MgCl2, 10% glycerol, 6mM BME, pH 7.5
  5. SEC200 buffer: 50mM HEPES, 500mM KCl, 5mM MgCl2, 6mM BME, pH 7.5
