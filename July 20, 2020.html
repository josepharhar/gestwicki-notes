DJB1 CTDs competition FP with FL Hsc70:
-GOAL: test a single dose of Hsc70 for the ability to compete with Hsp72 10mer tracer for binding to DJB1 CTDs
-conditions: 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 167uM DTT, 0.00167% Triton, pH 8
-due to large assay volume from undiluted Hsc70, final [DTT] and % Triton were lower than standard assay conditions; in the future, this can be compensated to match standard conditions
-tested conditions:
  1. Tracer alone
  2. Tracer + DJB1 CTDs
  3. Tracer + FL Hsc70
  4. Tracer + DJB1 CTDs + FL Hsc70
-RESULTS: at 86.8uM Hsc70 was able to compete away the tracer from DJB1 CTDs; at this single dose, no binding was observed between Hsc70 and the tracer
