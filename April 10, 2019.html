DJA1 purification day 2:
- GOAL: reverse purify away the TEV, run cleaved DJA1 on SEC200 to remove low molecular weight contaminants and high molecular weight aggregates
- reverse purification was performed as previously described; a sample of each fraction was tested by gel to determine cleavage efficiency and the location of DJA1 in solution (see attached)
- flowthrough was concentrated and injected onto SEC200, and relevant fractions were assessed by gel (see attached)
- after confirming that the majority of DJA1 had eluted during the reverse purification (rather than being present in the flowthrough/SEC200 input), TEV cleavage was performed again on DJA1, this time for ~2.5 hours at 34C
- after 34C TEV cleavage, protein was dialyzed into Ni-NTA compatible buffer for reverse purification
- RESULTS: due to failed TEV cleavage, the majority of DJA1 was left uncleaved and was not injected onto SEC200
