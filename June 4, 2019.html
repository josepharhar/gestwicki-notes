DnaJB1 expression:
- GOAL: express DJB1 from 4L of Rosetta culture
- to each L of TB, added 1mL 100mg/mL amp stock and 10mL of overnight culture
- cultures were incubated at 37C, 180RPM until OD600 ~0.8 was reached
- cultures were cooled to 18C, 160RPM for 1 hr, and then induced with 500uM IPTG and incubated overnight
- 1mL of culture was removed before induction, pelleted, and stored at -20C for future assessment of DJB1 expression
