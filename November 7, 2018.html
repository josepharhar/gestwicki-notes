Overnight cultures:
- GOALS: prepare large-scale O/N cultures for DJB1 for large-scale expression; prepare small-scale O/N cultures for DJA1, DJA2 Delta CT, and EB3 for preparation of glycerol stocks
- 25mL of TB was combined with 25uL of 100mg/mL amp in two 125mL E-flasks, colonies were picked from DJB1 plate
- for DJA1, DJA2 Delta CT, and EB3, 3mL of TB was combined with 3uL of respective antibiotic (kan for DJA1, amp for DJA2 Delta CT and EB3), colonies were picked
- all were grown overnight at 37C, 200 RPM
