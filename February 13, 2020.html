DJB4 competition FP:
-GOALS: 1. test the dependence of max mP signal on presence of reducing agent; 2. test ala scan peptides of Hsp72
-conditions: buffer conditions were the same as previously described, with addition of 1mM DTT
  • max mP: in wells with tracer +/- DJB4, 250uM Hsp72 10mer was added to see dynamic range in the presence of DTT
  • ala scan: peptides were tested in 8pt 3-fold dilution series as previously described; tested GSGPTIEEVA, GSGPTIEEAD, GSGPTIEAVD, GSGPTIAEVD
-results: addition of 1mM DTT seems to have increased the max mP signal (similar to values previously observed ~140-150); all ala scan peptides tested seemed to compete away tracer to a similar degree to each other and to previously tested WT peptide; V->A point mutant may have slightly increased affinity to DJB4, possibly due to removal of a hydrophobic residue that is highly solvent exposed; normalization was performed by setting 0% tracer bound as the mean signal from tracer alone (no protein, no peptide), and 100% as the max mean for each group
