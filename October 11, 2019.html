FP: Saturation binding of DJB4 to Hsp72 10mer tracer under reduced salt conditions
- GOAL: determine whether decreasing salt can increase the affinity of DJB4 to the tracer, allowing it to be a better tool for other assays (ex: FP competition)
- assay conditions: 10nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 0.01% Triton, varying protein concentration
- 15 min incubation at RT in the dark
- RESULTS: reduced salt did shift the binding curve, suggesting a higher affinity dependent upon polar interactions; however, the protein seemed unstable at high concentrations in this buffer—it was soluble in the experiment, but turned cloudy when it was left on ice after 0.1 micron filtration
