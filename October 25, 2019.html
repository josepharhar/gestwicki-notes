DJB4 competition FP:
- GOAL: compete away Hsp72 10mer FP probe using a dilution series of either Hsp72 10mer or Hsp90 alpha 10mer
- final conditions: 5uM DJB4, 10nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 0.01% Triton, pH 8 (and in the case of Hsp72 peptide competitions, 0.5% DMSO)
- incubated ~30 min
- RESULTS: Hsp72 10mer was able to compete away the probe, but a plateau was not reached; Hsp90 alpha peptide did not seem to compete away tracer under the tested conditions
