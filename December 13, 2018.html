DJA1 purification day 1:
- all steps performed as described by JDP purification protocol, except with modified purification buffers
- buffers were prepared using 5mM BME, 20uM ZnCl2, and 5% glycerol; see attached for calculations
- DJA1 was purified with high yield and minimal loss by Ni-NTA resin (see attached)
- incubated DJA1 elutions (~20mL each) with 5-6 aliquots of TEV for ~5.5 hrs at room temp
- dialyzed overnight into modified His-binding buffer
