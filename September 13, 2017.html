Dialysis of K18 P301L and Hsp72 SBD:
- approx 2 mL of K18 P301L was transferred into a 0.5-3mL 3.5 kDa cutoff dialysis cassette, and allowed to dialyze into 1L of DPBS with 2mM MgCl2 and 1mM DTT; allowed to spin in the cold room overnight
- approx 1.5 mL of Hsp72 SBD was transferred into a 0.5-3mL 3.5 kDa cutoff dialysis cassette, and allowed to dialyze into 500 mL of DPBS with 2mM MgCl2; allowed to spin in the cold room overnight
- though it was originally noted, buffers were not filtered
