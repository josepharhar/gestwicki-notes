Observations: ThT and PBB-5 seemed to elicit stronger signal when incubated with P301L fibrils.
 
Major caveats:
Quantification was based on SDS-PAGE, which presumably could not distinguish monomers, aggregated materials that can not be identified by the dyes and aggregated materials that can be identified by the dyes.
Two batches of fibrils were stored very differently, making it hard to conclude if differences observed were due to batch-to-batch variation or different storage conditions.
 
Next steps:
Prepare fibrils using two batches of monomers in parallel.
Repeat SDS-PAGE quantification and dye quantifications of freshly prepared fibrils.
Spin the materials down onto 96-well µclear plate, perform dye staining and microscopy.
