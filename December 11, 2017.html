Transform P301L plasmid into BL21 cells:
- see attached for protocol and notes
- added 0.5uL plasmid to approx 50uL BL21 cells
- incubated 5 min on ice, 45 sec heat shock, then 2 min on ice before adding 1mL LB media
- incubated with shaking at 37C for 1 hr
- after 1 hr, added 100uL or 200uL to prewarmed kan plates, respectively
- incubate overnight at 37C
