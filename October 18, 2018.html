Pellet and store bacterial cultures expressing DJA2-2-1 and DJA2 CT:
- 1mL of each respective culture (DJA2-2-1 or DJA2 CT) was collected and pelleted by centrifugation at max speed for 1 min
- remaining volume of each culture was pelleted by centrifugation at 4000RPM, 4C for 10 min
- pellets were then transferred to falcon tubes and stored at -80C until future use (2L per falcon tube, 1 tube for DJA2-2-1 and 1 tube for DJA2 CT)

Diagnostic gel for protein expression:
- see attached for details
- uninduced bacterial pellets were resuspended in 100uL 1X SDS sample buffer, while induced bacterial pellets were resuspended in 200uL 1X SDS sample buffer
- samples were then boiled for 5 min, then centrifuged at max speed for 5 min
- for each uninduced sample, 8uL of sample was loaded onto gel
- for induced samples, 5, 10, and 12uL of each sample were loaded onto the gel
- RESULTS: it appears that both constructs were robustly induced and expressed; DJA2-2-1 runs slightly lower than 50kDa, as expected; DJA2 CT runs near 25-20 kDa, which is near the expected 18 kDa (post TEV cleavage, would expected higher mol weight before TEV cleavage)
