Expression of DJA2-2-1 and DJA2 CT:
- O/N cultures had some evaporation, so final vol of each was ~20mL
- to each L of TB, added 1mL 100mg/mL amp and 10mL O/N culture (one of the DJA2-2-1 liters had slightly less than 10mL added)
- starting at 9:20 AM, cultures were incubated at 37C, 180RPM
- OD600 was checked periodically, and at OD>0.8, cultures were then incubated at 18C 160RPM for 1 hr
- each L was induced with 500uM IPTG and allowed to incubate overnight
- prior to induction, 1mL of each respective culture (DJA2-2-1 or DJA2 CT) was collected, pelleted by centrifugation at max speed for 1 min, and then pellets were stored at -20C

Preparation of JDP purification buffers:
- see attached for details
- after preparation, all buffers were stored at 4C

Start O/N cultures:
- as there was not enough leftover volume from O/N cultures to prepare glycerol stocks, new O/N cultures were prepared
- each culture was 3mL LB + 3uL 100mg/mL amp + 1 picked colony from the previous plates for DJA2-2-1 and DJA2 CT
