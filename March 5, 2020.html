DJB4 competition FP:
-GOAL: test 8 peptides with single point mutations at the isoleucine position of the Hsp70 C-term sequence (GSGPTIEEVD)
-conditions: 5uM DJB4, 10nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 2.5% DMSO, 1mM DTT, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-DJB4 stock (in 50mM Tris, 300mM NaCl, 10% glycerol, pH 8) was directly diluted into the assay buffer
-tracer only control was prepared in buffer that also included a dilution of the above DJB4 buffer
-results: it seems that none of the peptides were able to compete away more than ~40% of the tracer, compared to as much as ~70% competed away (see 021320); however, raw data suggests that there was variation of ~10mP in the baseline, no peptide controls, which may have been due to the 30 minute incubation period not being long enough
