EB3 purification day 2:
- GOAL: reverse purify away TEV protease, continue EB3 purification as necessary
- overnight dialyzed sample was incubated with equilibrated Ni-NTA resin (~7.5mL packed) for 1hr at 4C with rocking
- after collecting the flowthrough, resin was washed with ~150mL His-binding buffer and eluted with ~40mL elution buffer
- EB3 was present at a very dilute concentration in the flowthrough, while TEV remained on the resin until elution by imidazole (see attached gelâ€”for this gel, all samples were boiled for 5 min, run on a 10% gel)
- flowthrough (~40mL) was concentrated using 10k MWCO centrifugal filters to a final volume of ~1.5mL
- BCA was performed with undiluted EB3
- two ~1mL fractions were flash frozen and stored at -80C
- RESULTS: undiluted EB3 was measured to be ~7uM in solution; the drop in concentration between primary Ni-NTA elution and reverse TEV purification may be due to instability during room temp cleavage (or something else)
