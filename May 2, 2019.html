DJB1 FP repeat:
- GOAL: repeat DJB1 FP to assess binding to Hsp72 c-term FP probe; changes include increased DJB1 concentration and decreased salt concentration, in hopes of observing binding saturation for calculation of Kd
- to increase DJB1 concentration and decrease salt concentration, DJB1 was diluted 1:1 with 50mM Tris, pH 8 (to yield final salt concentration of 150mM) and then concentrated to approx half of the original volumeÂ using 10K MWCO spin filters
- BCA was used to determine final DJB1 concentration (169.34uM) (see attached)
- experimental setup: 20nM final tracer in 50mM Tris, 100mM NaCl, pH 8 + 0.01% Triton + 2-fold dilutions of DJB1, starting at ~112uM; final assay volume of 18uL
- after performing 2-fold dilutions of DJB1, 12uL of DJB1 was combined with 6uL of 60nM tracer + 0.03% Triton in 50mM Tris, pH 8
- after incubating plate 15min RT in the dark, plate was read using default FP settings on SpectraMax M5 plate reader
- RESULTS: binding was not saturated, but FP was successfully performed
