DJB1 CTDs competition FP:
-GOAL: test N-terminal truncations of Hsp72 peptide to determine minimum length for binding to DJB1 CTDs construct
-conditions: 5uM DJB1 CTDs, 20nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 1mM DTT, 2.5% DMSO, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-peptides were tested in 8 pt dose, 3-fold dilutions from 250uM
-RESULTS: as observed for DJB4 FL construct, 5mer peptide is unable to compete with tracer for binding; however, 6mer competes similarly to 10mer at highest dose; as previously observed, 7 residues is the minimum for significant competition, and the 7mer and 8mer peptides (possibly even 9mer) compete better than the WT 10mer
