  1. DJA2 elution profile by A280 shifted in the presence of monomeric tau. New peak appeared, likely corresponding to monomeric tau peak; however, the molar masses of these respective peaks are different (tau monomer alone ~48kDa, DJA2 + tau ~78kDa), suggesting that perhaps the peak appearing in the DJA2+tau run is a mixture of different species (tau alone and DJA2 dimers or mixed tau/DJA2 species?). Additionally, the relative ratios between peaks may be changed; it seems that in the presence of monomeric tau, there is a lower relative signal in the void and a higher signal corresponding to approx dimer.
  2. In the presence of tau fibrils, relative amount of protein in the void volume (~6mL) decreased, while a peak emerged at the very end of the run, likely corresponding to fibrillar tau peak (shown in red).
