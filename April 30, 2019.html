DJB1 FP:
- GOAL: perform fluorescence polarization to assess binding of full length DJB1 to Hsp72 C-term tracer (from Victoria’s stocks)
- experimental setup: combine DJB1 and FP tracer (20nM final) in buffer + 0.01% Triton at a final volume of 18uL; incubate for 15 min at RT in the dark; read plate (according to standard FP settings on SpectraMax M5)
- prep: from a 100uM stock of DJB1 in 50mM Tris, 300mM NaCl, pH 8, perform 14 2-fold dilutions; combine 9uL of respective DJB1 stock with 9uL of 40nM tracer in 50mM Tris, pH 8 + 0.02% Triton
- RESULTS: binding between DJB1 and tracer was detectable, but unsaturated
