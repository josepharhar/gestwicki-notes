EB3 8L expression:
- GOAL: express 8L of EB3 Rosetta cells for a higher yield protein purification
- to each of 8L of autoclaved TB, added 1mL 100mg/mL amp and 10mL of overnight starter culture
- incubated cultures at 37C, 180RPM for ~4.5 hrs, until OD600 reached 0.8
- cooled cultures to 18C, 160RPM for 1 hr before collecting a 1mL sample and inducing with 600uM IPTG (using 600uL of 1M IPTG stock)
- cultures incubated overnight
- 1mL sample culture was pelleted by centrifugation at max speed for 1 min and pellet was stored at -20C for future use

EB3 purification buffers:
- GOAL: prepare 1L of each relevant EB3 purification buffer for use in purification of the 8L prep
- buffers were prepared as described on 010719
  1. His binding buffer: 50mM Tris, 10mM imidazole, 500mM NaCl, pH 8
  2. Wash buffer 1: 50mM Tris, 30mM imidazole, 500mM NaCl, pH 8
  3. Wash buffer 2: 50mM Tris, 30mM imidazole, 100mM NaCl, pH 8
