Buffer preparation for DJC17 purification:
- GOAL: prepare conservative buffers for purification of DJC17, in the hopes of increasing yield and ATPase stimulation activity
- all buffers (except SEC) to include 5% glycerol and 6uM BME
- wash buffers did not include any imidazole, since the protein eluted too early in previous attempts
- based on publications of yeast homologue Cwc23, maximum salt for any buffer was decreased to 300mM
- SEC200 step was added to (hopefully) increase purity and assess quality/presence of aggregates; SEC200 buffer included 150mM NaCl and 6uM BME
- all buffers prepared by David Allegakoen, from the lab of Amit Sabnis
