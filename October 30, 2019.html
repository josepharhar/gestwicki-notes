DJB4 FP: single point tracer test
- GOAL: test a variety of mutant FP probes (from Victoria’s paper, Biochemistry 2015) for interaction with B4 under conditions used for 102519 competition assay
- final assay conditions: 5uM DJB4, 10nM tracer in 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
- tracers tested: 
  1. Hsp72 10mer (positive control)
  2. Hsp72 I->M (swap with Hsp90)
  3. Hsp90 alpha M->I (swap with Hsp72)
  4. Hsc70 ESG (phosphomimetic)
  5. Hsc70 SEG (phosphomimetic)
  6. Hsc70 PEI (phosphomimetic)
- RESULTS: delta mP is observed for the positive control and each of the Hsp72 phosphomimetic tracers. Delta mP is not observed for either of the M/I mutants; notably, both of these mutant tracers have much higher baseline mP, suggestive of tracer aggregation
