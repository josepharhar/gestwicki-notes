DJB1 CTDs competition FP with remaining P5 unnatural aa substitution:
-GOAL: test final P5 substitution (4-NO2-Phe) and retest 8mer truncation to compare potency with 10mer WT
-conditions: 5uM DJB1 CTDs, 20nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 1mM DTT, 2.5% DMSO, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-RESULTS: 8mer was indeed more potent than 10mer; the P5 substitution behaved similarly to the WT 10mer; large standard deviations for all peptides at the highest concentrations, perhaps due to insolubility/aggregation
