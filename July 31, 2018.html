Culturing Delta DnaK expression strain from the Mayer lab:
- RESULTS: after ~15-16 hrs at 30C, growth was observed in liquid culture (however, not very cloudy/low OD)
- two glycerol stocks were prepared by combining 700uL 60% glycerol with 700uL of the culture and stored at -80C in “Hsp70” box
- an inoculating loop was used to dip into the culture and streak on a fresh kan/chlor plate, which was left at RT on the bench
- remaining O/N culture stored at 4C
