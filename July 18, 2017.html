BCA for dialyzed tau concentration:
- used special kit for proteins in buffers with reducing agents (there’s DTT in tau/assay buffer)
- did BSA dilutions as described in the kit’s protocol (2 mg/mL, 1.5, 1, 0.5, 0.25, 0.125) and then did 1:4 dilutions, followed by 1:1 dilutions for WT and A15T tau
- used spectramax software on the computer in the lab to calculate a standard curve for the BSA and calculate concentrations of the unknowns (saved as excel sheet on lab server)
- RESULTS: WT is ~9 mg/mL, A152T is ~11 mg/mL
