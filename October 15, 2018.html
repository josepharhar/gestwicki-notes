Transformation of DJA2-2-1 and DJA2 CT into Rosetta competent cells:
- 1uL of DJA2-2-1 #1 pMCSG7 and DJA2 CT #1 pMCSG7 were transformed into 25uL commercial Rosetta competent cells, respectively
- 5 min ice, 45 sec heat shock, 2 min ice, added 1mL LB, recovered ~10 min, then plated 10uL of each onto prewarmed amp plates
- grown O/N at 37C
