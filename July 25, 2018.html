Preparation of Kanamycin/Chloramphenicol plates:
- GOAL: prepare plates with correct antibiotics to culture the delta DnaK strain from the Mayer lab from filter paper
- see attached
- LB-agar was prepared by adding agar to 1X LB media at 20g/L, then autoclaved on liquids 30 setting
- once LB-agar was cooled enough to touch, 50mg/mL kan stock and 34mg/mL chlor stock was added
- 15 mL was added to each of 12 plates
- note: bubbles present on many plates
- left on bench at room temp overnight
