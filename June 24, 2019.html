DSF for Delta Tm DJB1 +/- Hsp72 10mer:
- GOAL: perform DSF using sypro orange to test for the ability of Hsp72 10mer peptide to stabilize DJB1 (by changing the Tm)
- triplicate wells of 15uL total reaction were assembled for each of the following conditions:
  1. buffer only
  2. peptide only
  3. protein only
  4. protein + peptide
- final concentrations in assay: 20uM DJB1, 250uM peptide, 0.5% DMSO in 50mM Tris, 50mM NaCl pH 8.0, 0.01% Triton
- Taia provided a positive control (lysozyme)
- DSF was performed, ramping 1C per min from 25C to 95C
- RESULTS: Delta Tm was observed of ~1C
