EB3 test expression:
- GOAL: assess EB3 induction with a gradient of IPTG under overnight expression conditions
- from each overnight culture, 1mL of culture was pelleted and the supernatant discarded
- uninduced culture pellet (collected 011819) was resuspended in 100uL of 1X SDS sample buffer, while induced culture pellets were resuspended in 200uL of 1X SDS sample buffer
- all samples were boiled 5 min and centrifuged at max speed for 5 min
- for each sample, a gradient was loaded onto the gel to improve the resolution of specific bands (see attached)
- RESULTS: at 600uM IPTG and greater, a band appeared at the expected ~30kDa of EB3, suggesting that protein expression was induced; however, this was not a very robust protein expression, since the band was not significantly darker/larger than those for endogenous E. coli proteins
