DJB1 CTDs competition FP DMSO titration:
-GOAL: test a wide range of DMSO concentrations in competition FP to establish the tolerance of the assay/protein to DMSO; this information can be used for future peptide NMR studies
-conditions: 5uM DJB1 CTDs, 40nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 1mM DTT, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-tested DMSO concentrations (final in assay): 0.5%, 2.5%, 5%, 10%, 20%, 30%, 40%, 50%
-most concentrated Hsp72 peptide stock (50mM) was used to prepare a dilution series for each of the above DMSO concentrations
-Hsp72 10mer was tested in 4pt dose, 3-fold dilutions starting from 250uM final in assay
-for each DMSO concentration, triplicate controls were performed with no peptide, and tracer alone
-RESULTS: in each of the tested DMSO concentrations, the highest peptide concentration resulted in a decrease in polarization; when normalized, the decrease in % tracer bound seems similar at highest peptide concentration; at increasing DMSO concentrations (10% or more), only the highest peptide dose resulted in a decrease in mP
