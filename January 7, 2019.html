Results of DnaJA2 pGEM-T transformation:
- many colonies present after ~13 hours of incubation
- stored plate at 4C until further use

EB3 expression:
- to each of 4 L of TB, added 1mL 100mg/mL amp and 20mL of EB3 Rosetta O/N culture
- incubated at 37C 180RPM starting at 8:15 AM
- at OD600 ~0.6 (10:15AM), expression of EB3 was induced by addition of 1mL of 1M IPTG (1mM final)
- cultures were incubated at 37C 180RPM for 4 hours (10:24AM-2:24PM)
- cultures were spun at 4000RPM for 15 minutes, and pellet were stored (2L per 50mL falcon tube) at -80C
- to collect pre- and post-induction samples for further analysis, 1mL of culture was removed, pelleted by 1 min max speed centrifugation, and stored at -20C
