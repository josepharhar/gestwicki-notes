FP: Saturation binding of DJB1 and DJB4 to Hsp90 alpha 10mer tracer
- GOAL: determine if these JDPs can bind to the c-terminus of Hsp90alpha, and if so, how the affinity compares to Hsp72
- assay conditions: 20nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 50mM NaCl, 0.01% Triton, pH 8, variable protein concentration
- RESULTS: DJB4 demonstrated an ability to bind to the tracer, but much more weakly than to the Hsp72 10mer tracer; DJB1 did not show binding, but it was present at lower concentrations in the assay than B4
