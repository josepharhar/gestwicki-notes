DJA1 purification day 1:
- GOALS: lyse bacteria, affinity purify DJA1 with Ni-NTA resin, TEV cleave, and dialyze into Ni-NTA compatible buffer
- cultures were pelleted by centrifugation at 4000RPM for 15 min
- pellets were resuspended in lysis buffer (as described in JDP purification protocol) supplemented with xxx DNase and RNase (to remove nucleic acids which might be binding to DJA1 and acting as a template for aggregation)
- further purification steps were performed as previously described, with the addition of xxx DNase and RNase during the incubation with Ni-NTA resin and the incubation with TEV
- samples of each purification fraction were run on a gel, showing relatively pure DJA1 (see attached); unexpectedly large number of low molecular weight bands were present
- RESULTS: DJA1 was purified and soluble with relative purity
