DnaJB1 purification day 1:
- GOAL: affinity purify DJB1 from lysate, TEV cleave to remove His tag, and dialyze overnight into Ni-NTA resin compatible buffer
- affinity purification was performed as previously described
- affinity purification fractions were assessed by SDS-PAGE (see attached)
- TEV cleavage was performed at 34C for 1hr
- protein was then dialyzed overnight into 4L of His binding buffer
- RESULTS: DJB1 was purified to relatively high purity, with some low molecular weight contaminants
