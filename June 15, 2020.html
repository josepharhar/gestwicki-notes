DJB1 CTDs competition FP:
-GOAL: test a selection of P5 substituted 10mer peptides for competition with the WT Hsp72 10mer tracer
-peptides tested: P5 substituted with the following aa’s—L, P, G, C, D, E, N, Q, V
-conditions: 5uM DJB1 CTDs, 20nM tracer, 50mM Tris, 15mM NaCl, 1mM DTT, 2.5% DMSO, 0.01% Triton, pH 8
-RESULTS: the majority of peptides cannot compete with the tracer; notably, valine substitution seems to significantly increase affinity, while leucine substitution trends more closely with the rest of the tested peptides; very large standard deviation for valine substitution at the highest tested concentration, likely due to insolubility
