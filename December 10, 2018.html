DJB4 purification day 3:
- GOAL: purify DJB4 by SEC200 column, concentrate, BCA, and flash freeze aliquots for future use
- DJB4 in His-binding buffer + 5% glycerol (supplemented) was injected onto SEC200, exchanged into JDP buffer (50mM Tris, 300mM NaCl pH 8),Â with the majority of the protein eluted at the approximate dimer volume (see attached SEC trace)
- as shown by gel (see attached) the majority of the protein was present in the dimer fractions, was of high purity, with only a smaller molecular weight impurity present
- combined dimer fractions (C7-C12), spiked in 5% glycerol, and concentrated using 10K MWCO centrifugal units
- BCA for concentration: started with 1:4 dilution and performed further 2-fold dilutions; protein was measured to be ~184uM (see attached)
- protein was frozen in small aliquots (~200uL each)
