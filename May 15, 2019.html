DJA2 CTDI single domain cloning:
- GOAL: design primers to create a single domain CTDI construct, starting from DJA2 Delta CT pMCSG7 vector
- primers were designed to overlap with the TEV cleavage site, then skip the J domain and linker, and finally continue at the start of CTDI (marked in the plasmid map on the server)
- primer sequences:
  1. Forward: 5’-CTGTACTTCCAATCCAATGCTAATCAGAGTAGAAGTCGAAATGGC-3’
  2. Reverse complement: 5’-GCCATTTCGACTTCTACTCTGATTAGCATTGGATTGGAAGTACAG-3'
