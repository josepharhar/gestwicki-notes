DJB1 purification day 1:
- GOAL: purify His-tagged DJB1 for the purpose of a DNA-encoded library screen with GSK; because immobilization via a tag is required for this screen, the His tag will not be removed
- lysed cells, equilibrated Ni-NTA resin, incubated with resin, washed, and eluted as previously described (JDP purification protocol)
- in parallel, SEC200 column was equilibrated with filtered JDP buffer (50mM Tris, 300mM NaCl)
- induced and uninduced cell lysates, as well as 10uL of each step of purification, were analyzed by SDS-PAGE
- elutedÂ DJB1 was concentrated to 5mL total and injected onto SEC200 column for removal of low molecular weight contaminants and large protein aggregates
- RESULTS: the majority of expressed DJB1 was isolated by affinity purification; SEC200 trace suggests that a DJB1 aggregates were separated from dimeric DJB1
