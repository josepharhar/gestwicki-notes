Transformation of DnaJA2 pGEM-T plasmid:
- transformed 1uL of resuspended commercial plasmid into 1 aliquot of Nova Blue cells (~10min on ice, 45 sec 42C heat shock, 2 min on ice, 20min recovery in 1mL LB 37C)
- plated 50uL onto prewarmed amp plate, incubated O/N

Starter culture for EB3 expression:
- inoculated 90mL TB + 90uL of 100mg/mL amp using EB3 Rosetta glycerol stock
- incubated O/N at 37C
